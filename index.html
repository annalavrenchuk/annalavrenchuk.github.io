Curriculum Vitae

I am an undergraduate student at Harvard University, concentrating in Applied Math on a Government track with a secondary in Environmental Science and Public Policy. At Harvard, I work with Professor Brian DeLay at the Radcliffe Institute for Advanced Study, helping build a global database of 19th-century arms and ammunition trade to understand how industrialization shaped global inequality and power. I also assisted Professor Hannah Riley Bowles at the Harvard Kennedy School on research examining gender dynamics in peace negotiations. Beyond academia, I contributed to projects advancing marine protection in Latin America and clean energy transitions in Ukraine.
